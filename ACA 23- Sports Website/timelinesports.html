A Journey through the marvellous carrer of Jasprit Bumrah

  • 2014 Jasprit Bumrah made his debut in the Indian Premier League at just 19-years of age. He picked as many as three wickets only in his first match, becoming the second bowler in the history of the tournament to achieve the feat. Having been brought back by Mumbai Indian in 2014, Bumrah has been a mainstay for them ever since.
  • 27th January 2016 Jasprit Bumrah made his debut in the International cricket on 27th January 2016 against Australia.
  • 2017 Bumrah was named in the ODI XI of the year by Cricbuzz.
  • 2018 He was named in the ODI XI of the year by ESPN CricInfo and Test XI and ODI XI by Cricbuzz.
  • 2018 He became the first Asian bowler to pick five wicket-hauls in Australia, England, and South Africa
